ABOUT KERALAVISION BROADBAND LIMITED


Keralavision Broadband Limited, is an initiative of independent Cable TV Operators in Kerala under
the guidance of Cable Operators Association ( COA). COA is an umbrella union of local cable
operators all over Kerala. It is a conglomeration of more than 4000 independent cable networks
functioning all over Kerala; its main objective is to develop Cable TV Industry of Kerala by means
of building wider networks, upgrading technology, finding new avenues of activity etc apart
from addressing various issues and challenges before the industry for and on behalf of its members.
